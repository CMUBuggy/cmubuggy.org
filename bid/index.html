Reserve Bids

Placing a reserve bid is the equivilant to saying "I'm willing to pay up to this amount" - anytime someone else makes a bid, the auction will automatically bid one higher than that (assuming your reserve bid is greater than the new bid ). For example : if you have placed a reserve bid of 20 dollars, and the current high bid is 10 dollars, an 11 dollar bid will automatically be placed for you. If someone else makes a 12 dollar bid, a 13 dollar bid will automatically be placed for you.

Once you have placed a reserve bid, that amount will be noted in the "Reserve Bid" section at the top of the auction. Note that it is still possible to place a normal bid while you have a reserve bid placed - if you are the highest in an auction, the reserve bid will not force an automatic bid against yourself.
