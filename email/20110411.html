Lead Truck Auction Auction starts Tomorrow at 9am and runs until 9pm Wednesday
For the third year, Sweepstakes has given us the opportunity to raise funds for the Buggy Alumni Association by auctioning off the chance to ride in the lead truck during live races. Auction winners get to ride in the bed of the pickup truck with the head judge, Sweepstakes chairman, and other race officials. The first $1,000 from the auction go directly to help pay for the dual jumbotrons on the course. Join in on the fun and help us make our #1 fundraiser a success again this year!

Check out our all-new website
We finished up our months-long project renovating cmubuggy.org just in time for raceday, and if you haven't checked it out yet, you need to. The biggest new feature is a library of videos including all cmuTV coverage for the last 8 years and a collection of other footage. A new wiki-style reference section, and improved history database promise to help organize and preserve buggy information. Last but not least, a content feed on the homepage tells you exactly where to look for the freshest content.

Not going to make it to the 'Burgh?
We know that not everyone can make it, so we do our best to bring the raceday experience to out-of-town fans. Make sure to visit cmubuggy.org on raceday for:
  • Extensive reports of rolls and breaking developments in the news section
  • Streaming video from cmuTV with audio by WRCT and our alumni broadcasters
  • Immediate leaderboard updates directly from the official timing system
  • Chat with other far flung fans in our "Raceday Buzz" chat room
  • Submit questions for the broadcasters to answer on air between heats
  • Full pusher, driver, and buggy details for every team
